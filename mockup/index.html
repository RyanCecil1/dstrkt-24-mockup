Brand Story

A sharper mood, a clearer next step, the same destination.

Dstrkt 24 already has the ingredients of a stronger digital presence: an identifiable logo, active social presence, direct contact paths, and a menu with broad category depth. What it lacks is curation. This concept gives the venue an arrival moment before the menu, so guests feel the brand first and browse second.

Warm contrast palette Editorial typography Mobile-first CTA flow Menu linked to live source
What Changed

No auto-popup. No dead-end gallery. No guessing.

The redesign leads with a hero, promotes a current signature item, surfaces the live menu by category, then brings in contact, location, Instagram, and feedback as obvious actions. It still respects the current ecosystem, but packages it like a real hospitality brand.

Visit and Contact

Everything actionable, all in one clean strip.

These actions are grounded in the live parent site and linked properties. The difference is presentation: they now feel deliberate, not buried.
